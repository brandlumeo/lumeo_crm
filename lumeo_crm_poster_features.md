# Lumeo CRM: Features for Poster Design

### 📊 **Sales & Pipeline Management**
*   **Visual Sales Pipeline (Kanban):** Drag-and-drop deal tracking across customizable stages (Discovery, Proposal, Won, Lost).
*   **Lead & Contact Management:** Seamlessly track relationships from initial prospect to paying customer with detailed profiles, activity logs, and notes.
*   **Dashboard & Analytics:** Real-time command center displaying total revenue, open deals, active tasks, and new leads.

### 🏢 **HR & Team Operations**
*   **Attendance & Leave Tracking:** Employee self-service portal for clocking in/out, logging breaks, and requesting time off (Paid, Sick, Casual).
*   **Expense & Asset Management:** Submit and approve out-of-pocket expense claims, and track company hardware (laptops, phones) assigned to staff.
*   **Payroll & Salary Slips:** Generate beautiful, downloadable PDF payslips with basic salary, allowances, and deductions.
*   **Task Management:** Assign actionable items with deadlines, track progress ("In Progress" / "Done"), and send automated daily reminders.

### 🤝 **Customer Success & Billing**
*   **Customer Support Ticketing:** A shared inbox for support tickets submitted via the customer portal, complete with assignment and status tracking.
*   **Quotes & Invoicing:** Create professional pricing proposals and payment requests, calculate totals automatically, and send secure PDF links directly to clients.
*   **Calendar & Bookings:** Calendly-style public booking links that integrate with Google Calendar to automatically schedule meetings and generate Google Meet links.

### 🚀 **Marketing & AI Features**
*   **Bulk Email & Campaigns:** Create email templates and launch mass marketing campaigns to targeted segments (e.g., "All Leads") using integrated SMTP.
*   **AI Chat Assistant:** A built-in, intelligent dashboard widget that instantly answers natural language questions about your CRM data (e.g., *"How many leads did we get today?"*).

### ⚙️ **Platform & SaaS Capabilities**
*   **Customer Portal:** A dedicated, secure space for clients to view invoices, download files, and submit support tickets.
*   **Team & Role Management:** Invite staff with specific access levels (Admin vs. Member).
*   **Automated SaaS Billing:** Seamless checkout and subscription management powered by Razorpay.
